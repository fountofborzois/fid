Hello! My name's Rebecca Looney. I'm currently a student at Seminole State College of Florida working on my Associates in Science degree on Digital Media. My interests have bounced back and forth between voice acting, animating, concept artist, and after years of consideration, I decided I wanted to become a game developer. I've been drawing and doodling my entire life on various programs and forms of media, however after taking some classes at Seminole State College of Florida, I've learned that I enjoy programming and decided that's what I want to do. During my time at SSC, I've created a multiplayer game with multiple minigames that keeps scores even if you switch games, as well as a food ordering app simulator. I love to create and build off of ideas. I belive my skillset would be an asset to any project and help it excel.
